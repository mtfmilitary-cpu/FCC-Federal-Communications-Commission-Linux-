International continuous wave (CW) rules and dictionary.

Standard Signal Timing Protocol

  • Dot / Dit (•): The baseline unit of transmission speed.
  • Dash / Dah (—): Lasts exactly three times longer than a dot.
  • Letter Spacing: The silence between elements of one letter is 1 dot.
  • Word Spacing: The silence between separate words is 7 dots.
